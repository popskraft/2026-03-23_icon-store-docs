# SPEC-6: Storefront IA & SEO

Status: Skeleton — Phase 2
Parent: `MASTER-ARCHITECTURE-v4.md` v4.3
Scope: BigCommerce storefront IA, navigation, filters, SEO structure, trust signals

---

## Часть A. Главное

### 0.1 Краткое резюме

- BigCommerce hosted storefront, no headless in v1.
- Amazon is the primary revenue channel; storefront is the owned parallel asset.
- Navigation and filters must stay intentionally small for MVP.

### 0.2 MVP direction

Navigation:
`Shop All | By Saint | By Purpose | Gifts | New Arrivals`

Max 4 filters:
1. Size
2. Material
3. Price
4. Technique, only if catalog quality supports it

---

## Часть B. Детали

## 1. Trust signals

Priority candidates:
- provenance
- blessing status, if factual and supportable
- material
- workshop or maker identity

Do not overload the storefront with secondary taxonomy before the pilot proves it helps.

---

## 2. Open work for Phase 2

1. Finalize category tree for MVP.
2. Freeze trust signal hierarchy.
3. Define landing-page SEO structure.
4. Decide where site should push direct checkout versus Amazon redirect.
